The Turtle and the Crab

HOW TO PLAY

Tobey the Turtle was thrilled when he was asked to participate in a midnight race. Unfortunately, his racing partner and comrade, Collin the Crab had consumed a curious cask of cactus juice and was acting erratic! As well as starting the race before the official countdown had begun, Collin has crazily crafted traps that only the most courageous of friends would face to reach their friend!

On your turn, you will be given a Clue and a number on the clue board. Each word on the game board represents Tobey's possible advance towards Collin. The number next to the Clue indicates how many words on the Clue Board, the Clue relates to. Choose correctly and Tobey will advance towards Collin. Choose incorrectly and you could fall into a trap. A trap slows you down, and allows Collin to steam ahead twice! Some Clue Board Words are misleading and lead to dead ends. But beware, one of Collins' traps is deadlier than the others!
